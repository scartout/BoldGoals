Tips:

1. Goal form define overall objective. Fill form with your big objective.

2. Always determine start date and scheduled end date. There's no-no time like the present.

3. Your goal cannot be impersonal - write "I finish with smoking" instead of "Stop smoking".

4. If you want to lose 15 kg, write "I'm loosing 25 kg" - bold goals boost your motivation more than normal goals.
